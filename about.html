Meet TJ Johnson

Hey, hey! I'm TJ Johnson, a dreamer with a suitcase and an unshakable thirst for adventure. From vibrant cityscapes to tranquil beaches, I've explored the world one destination at a time—all in the name of uncovering its hidden gems. Traveling solo has become my greatest teacher, revealing the beauty of independence, the joy of connection, and the magic that lies just beyond the horizon.

My Story

I grew up in Michigan, a state where natural beauty meets urban vibrancy. My childhood was spent exploring the Detroit Riverfront, skipping rocks along the Great Lakes, and wandering the trails of Belle Isle Park. Some of my fondest memories include riding the Boblo Island amusement park boat, feeling the cool breeze off the water, and daydreaming about faraway places. Even the Canada Tunnel entrance from Detroit fueled my early fascination with crossing borders and discovering the unknown.

From those early days in Michigan, my love for travel grew into a lifelong passion. My most recent adventure took me to the sun-soaked shores of Hawaii, where I spent my days embracing the island's serenity and culture. Imagine mornings filled with ocean breezes, afternoons hiking lush green valleys, and nights stargazing under the island sky. For me, Hawaii wasn't just another destination—it was a reminder of the transformative power of solo travel and the joy of finding connection in the unfamiliar.   

Now, after years of exploring, I've made a home base in Atlanta. This city, with its unique blend of modern energy and Southern charm, offers endless inspiration. From the bustling streets of Midtown to the peaceful trails of Piedmont Park, Atlanta gives me a sense of belonging while still feeding my wanderlust.

My Travel Philosophy

Through this blog, I want to inspire you to embrace the freedom of solo travel, take that leap into the unknown, and find the magic waiting just beyond your comfort zone. Whether you’re dreaming of wandering quiet beaches, exploring bustling cities, or simply taking a small step toward adventure, I’m here to share my journey, tips, and lessons along the way. My philosophy is simple: travel light, live fully, and never stop exploring.
